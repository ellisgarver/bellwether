Shale oil production impact on global oil markets

The rise of shale oil production in the United States significantly altered expectations about global crude oil shortages. Oil prices have fluctuated dramatically, influenced by technological advancements and geopolitical events such as the Russian invasion of Ukraine. These changes highlight the dynamic interplay between supply innovations and market forces.
