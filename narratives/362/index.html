African vaccine manufacturing gaps

African countries face significant challenges in vaccine production, with less than 1 percent of vaccines used on the continent being locally manufactured. This vulnerability is highlighted by the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and previous health crises like H1N1, underscoring the need for regional responses and domestic support.
