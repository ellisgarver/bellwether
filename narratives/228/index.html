Top five journals in economics

The American Economic Review, Econometrica, the Journal of Political Economy, the Quarterly Journal of Economics, and the Review of Economic Studies dominate publication and influence research directions. Their importance for young economists' careers is widely recognized but little has changed in their publication processes over decades.
