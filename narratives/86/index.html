Capital controls in emerging markets

The debate on capital controls resurfaced after the Global Crisis and during the COVID-19 pandemic. Emerging market economies faced significant capital flow reversals, prompting discussions on whether to use exchange rate flexibility or capital controls to manage financial stability risks.
