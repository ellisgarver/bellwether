DHS human trafficking programs

The Department of Homeland Security manages several programs aimed at combating human trafficking. These include the Center for Countering Human Trafficking within Immigration and Customs Enforcement, which coordinates law enforcement efforts and victim assistance. The programs also receive funding to support anti-trafficking initiatives.
