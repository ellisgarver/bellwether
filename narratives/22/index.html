EU fiscal rules and expenditure

The Stability and Growth Pact enshrined fiscal rules in the European Union. These rules aimed to keep public debt and deficits low but are now seen as complex and insufficiently effective. Expenditure rules, while less common, are considered better for balancing budget discipline with economic stability.
