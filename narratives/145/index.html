Impact of immigration on native workers

Research from the NBER, VoxEU, IMF, and Brookings examines how migration affects employment opportunities for native-born individuals. Studies focus on labor market dynamics, particularly among low-skilled workers, and discuss policy implications in countries like the UK and the US.
