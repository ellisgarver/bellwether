European parliament elections voter turnout

The European Parliament elections in 2014 faced low voter participation, with turnout declining from 62% in 1979 to 43% in 2009. Analysts noted that protest parties performed well but did not significantly alter the political landscape, while the elections were seen as a referendum on European leadership's handling of crises.
