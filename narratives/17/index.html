AI adoption in US economy

The use of artificial intelligence by businesses is increasing, as shown by surveys from the Census Bureau. Adoption rates vary across industries and firms, with about 18% of U.S. companies now using AI technologies. Policymakers are considering the labor market impacts of this trend.
