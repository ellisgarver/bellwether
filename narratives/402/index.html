Government shutdowns in United States

The U.S. government has experienced multiple shutdowns, most recently from October 1, 2025 to November 30, 2025, impacting non-essential operations and raising concerns about governance and the economy. Shutdowns have occurred under various political circumstances, reflecting ongoing debates over funding and legislative processes.
