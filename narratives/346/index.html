Energy conservation standards legislation

Legislation proposed by the House Committee on Energy and Commerce and the Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources aimed at amending energy and water conservation standards for appliances, equipment, and electricity-generating facilities. The bills authorize DOE to issue or revise these standards and FERC to ensure reliable service.
