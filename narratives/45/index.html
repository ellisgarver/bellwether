Macroprudential policy and financial stability

Macroprudential policies and tools are discussed in relation to reducing systemic risk following the global financial crisis. These measures aim to stabilize the economy by addressing issues like asset bubbles and credit imbalances, rather than focusing solely on individual institutions.
