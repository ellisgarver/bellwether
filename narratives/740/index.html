School choice and educational outcomes

School choice policies allow parents to select schools beyond their local ones, aiming to improve student matching and performance. Despite theoretical benefits, empirical evidence from various countries is mixed, suggesting that while some prefer elite schools, the overall impact on education quality remains uncertain.
